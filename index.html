CIMPA Schools Recife 2026

About CIMPA Schools

CIMPA Schools aim to provide an introduction to current research in mathematics and related fields such as computer science and theoretical physics. The focus is on regions with a genuine enthusiasm for mathematics and potential for research development.

The CIMPA Schools Recife event will take place at the Universidade Federal Rural de Pernambuco (UFRPE), Brazil, and is scheduled for either January 19–31, 2026 or January 26–February 7, 2026. Participants from neighboring developing countries will receive financial support for travel and living expenses, as part of CIMPA's mission.

Program Overview

Our school spans two weeks and features four main courses, each covering essential aspects of complex systems and applied mathematics:

  • Mathematical Modeling in Neuroscience
  • Topological Data Analysis in Complex Systems
  • Mathematical Modeling of Epidemiological Systems
  • Statistical Mechanics of Neural Networks

Hands-on programming sessions, short communication slots for participants, and round-table discussions on ethics and research in the Global South are also planned.

Organization

The event is organized by:

  • Local Coordinator: Prof. Rubia Araújo (UFRPE)
  • External Coordinator: Dr. Fernando A. N. Santos (University of Amsterdam)
  • Committee Members: UFRPE, UFPE, SBMAC, and invited instructors from around the world.

Host Institution: Universidade Federal Rural de Pernambuco, Recife, Brazil.

Registration

The registration process is open to graduate students, early-career researchers, and anyone interested in advanced training in mathematics and related fields. A limited number of grants are available for participants from developing countries.

More details on how to apply will be announced soon.
