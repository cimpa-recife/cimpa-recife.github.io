Courses

🧠 Mathematical Neuroscience

Instructors: Ana P. Millán (University of Granada), Fernanda Selingardi (UFAL)

Keywords: Neural dynamics, synchronization phenomena, criticality in neural networks, brain network analysis, differential equations, dynamical systems

Practical Focus: Hands-on modeling of neural systems using Python, with applications to brain function and neural network analysis.

📊 Topological Data Analysis

Instructor: Fernando A.N. Santos (University of Amsterdam/UFPE)

Keywords: Persistent homology, computational topology, simplicial complexes, high-order interactions, real-world applications

Practical Focus: Analysis of complex datasets from neuroscience, epidemiology, and finance using Python libraries (GUDHI, Ripser).

🦠 Epidemiological Modeling

Instructors: Danilo Souza (BCAM), João Gondim (UFPE)

Keywords: Compartmental models, network analysis, stochastic processes, parameter estimation, disease control strategies

Practical Focus: Simulation of disease outbreaks using SIR/SEIR models and network-based approaches in Python.

⚛️ Statistical Mechanics of Neural Networks

Instructors: Elena Agliari, Adriano Barra (Sapienza Università di Roma)

Keywords: Spin-glass models, learning algorithms, Ising models, machine learning systems, neural network design

Practical Focus: Application of statistical mechanics principles to analyze and design neural networks.

Research Talks

Thematic Sessions of Short Communications

  • Local Expertise: Presentations by scholars from Recife and neighboring areas, highlighting regional research strengths in complex systems and applied mathematics.
  • Participant Contributions: Opportunities for attendees to present their own work, with dedicated time for discussion and feedback.
